FMWebFrame - Change Log

###

Version 13e (07-18-2014)

Complete rewrite and reorganization of FMWebFrame. 
* Added support for fmEvaluate function. 
* Added "Site Shield" function.
* Redesigned container publishing method.
* Redesigned fmExecuteSQL function, with support for new OFFSET and FETCH FIRST options.
* Redesigned demo Web app.
* Significantly simpler FMWebFrame script in the demo database.
